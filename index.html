Born of Night, Worn as Oath

2bleed 4 dis gen is a chapter — not a trend. We collect traces: textures, symbols, and whispers that outlast seasons. This is a space for visuals, words, and rituals in cloth and shadow. No storefronts, only statements.

The Manifesto

We choose the black not to hide but to declare. Each cut, each stitch, each emblem is a line in our language of dissent. We honour craft, discard fast fashion rituals, and curate imagery that holds weight. Wear it like a sigil.
